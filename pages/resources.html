Autodesk Fusion

A fantastic CAD/CAM package for fabrication and product design. Very approachable for beginners and lots of resources. Has a huge online community of makers while also being used for high level engineering. Great for 3D printing!

  • Autodesk Courses

Rhino

Rhino, or Rhinoceros 3D, is a powerful 3D modeling software used in various fields including architecture, industrial design, and digital fabrication. It's known for its precision, flexibility, and extensive plugin ecosystem.
